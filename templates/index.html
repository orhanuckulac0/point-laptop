How it works?

To check cafes, you don't need to Register and Log in. You can click the "Explore Cafes" button and check out our cafes list. If you want to share your favourite places, you need to register and suggest a cafe by clicking "Suggest Cafe" button. After you submit your suggestion, we will review and confirm it. If It's confirmed, your suggestion will be published.

Meet with other people

Our community is constantly growing. There's a high chance you will meet with people who are using this website. Very soon, we will enable user comments, user chat and user profiles. This way it will be easier for you to meet and work with people who are looking to find new places to drink coffee and work there. You better not miss out these!
